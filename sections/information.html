How to get to James Clerk Maxwell Building

The event will take place at JCMB on the King's Buildings campus.

The address is:
JCMB
Mayfield Rd
Edinburgh EH9 3FD
United Kingdom

To get to Edinburgh:

  • By plane: Edinburgh Airport is connected to the city by bus (AirLink) and tram.
  • By train: The closest station is Edinburgh Waverley in the city centre.
  • By car: The campus lies north of the Edinburgh Bypass (A720). Take the exit onto A701 and follow until Mayfield Road.
See also https://transport.ed.ac.uk/travelling-here/kings-buildings for transport information and a campus map.
