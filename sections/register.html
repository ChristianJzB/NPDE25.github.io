Travel and child care support

Limited funding is available to cover accommodation and travel expenses. To qualify, you need to be an early-career researcher, and be affiliated to an institution in the UK or the EU. To apply, please let us know during registration and send your CV to npde25@maths.ed.ac.uk.

A portion of our funding is dedicated to covering child care costs incurred while attending the summer school. Please indicate during the registration process whether you'd like to apply for support.
